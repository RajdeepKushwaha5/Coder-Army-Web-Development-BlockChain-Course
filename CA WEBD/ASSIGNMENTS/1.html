Biography of Nikola Tesla

Early Life

Nikola Tesla was born on July 10,1856,in Smiljan, which was part of Austrian Empire (modern-day Croatia).
His father was a Priest, and his mother was an inventor who encouraged his curiosity from young age.

Career

Tesla moved to the United States in 1884 to work for Thomas Edison. He later invented the alternating current(AC) electrical system , which laid the foundation for modern electricity distribution.
He also conducted pioneering experiments in wireless communication and X-rays.

Achievements

Tesla held over 300 patents during his lifetime.He is best known to the contribution of development of AC power , the Tesla coil,wireless technologies.
Despite his many contributions, Tesla faced financial difficulties later in life.

Legacy

Tesla's work has left a lasting impact on modern technology.His inventions have paved the way for many of the electrical systems we use today.
He is remembered as a visionary who was ahead of his time.
